About AL RAHA

Naseem Al Raha Documents Clearing is a trusted civil company based in Dubai, operating since 1994 with a valid trade license (No. 235513) issued by the Dubai Department of Economy and Tourism. Specializing in document clearing, photocopying, typing, and printing services, the company also holds permits for additional activities, including internet services and media distribution.

As a long-standing member of the Dubai Chamber of Commerce (Membership No. 31704), Al Raha adheres to UAE regulations, ensuring reliable and compliant services. Located in Al Bada’a, the company serves clients with efficiency and professionalism, backed by a certified e-document system for verification.
